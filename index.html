I am a current PhD candidate at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I am broadly interested in instrumentation and exoplanet high-contrast imaging, and am part of the SCALES (Slicer Combined with an Array of Lenslets for Exoplanet Spectroscopy) instrument team. SCALES is scheduled to be commissioned at on the Keck II telescope in 2026.

Interests:

  • Astronomical instrumentation
  • Optomechanical design, fabrication, integration, and testing
  • Exoplanet high-contrast imaging
  • Science pedagogy
